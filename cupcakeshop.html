CUPCAKE SHOP

The Cupcake Station was the first cupcake shop in Michigan and one of the very first in the country. We have been baking gourmet cupcakes,100% from Scratch, since day one. We always use the finest and most natural ingredients we can find. We had a hard time deciding which cupcakes to bake each day that we decided to bake 24 flavors everyday so our customers can have a variety of flavors to choose from. The Cupcake Station was also the first to create the Bump-a-licious Cupcake and it has become one of our best sellers and has even made the national news. You can't go wrong with a cupcakes dipped in Chocolate! Since we opened our first location in 2006, our cupcakes have been enjoyed by thousands including Martha Stewart, Bobby Flay and Michigan's former Governor Jennifer Granholm. And now with the launch of our new eCommerce website, we're very excited to be shipping cupcakes, so that many more canenjoy our cupcakes all over the country! .
